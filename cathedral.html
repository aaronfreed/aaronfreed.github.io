Tempus Irae Redux’s “Downward Spiral”: A Cathedral

Some in-editor screenshots of the central architectural feature of Tempus Irae Redux’s level “Downward Spiral”, which I’m posting as illustrative material for my level soundtrack commentary. James Hastings-Trew designed the interior and made all the textures and landscapes; I designed the exterior. Click any image to enlarge it.

I posted a gameplay preview on YouTube on 2024-01-04; however, several aspects of the level and gameplay have since changed, and the level music we were using at the time has been moved elsewhere in the game.

The numbers in the top-left corner are editor jargon, explained here. At some point I’ll take newer screenshots that don’t include them.
