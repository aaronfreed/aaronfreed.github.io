Marathon Phoenix Dynamic Range Logs

These are the dynamic range logs for the original and remastered versions of the soundtrack for Marathon Phoenix. They were originally generated with the TT Dynamic Range Meter for foobar2000; I’ve formatted them as HTML tables for improved legibility. This page is on my site because I remastered the soundtrack – I explain my process here. One of the results of my remastering process is (in many cases) improved dynamic range (DR), as seen by many tracks (and the overall album) having higher DR scores. On that note, DR, Peak, and RMS (Root Mean Square, the effective average amplitude of the track) are all expressed in decibels (dB).
