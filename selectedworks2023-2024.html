Introduction

As 2024 drew to a close, I realized I’d written some three and a half hours of music since that January. As a result, even though I’d made a sampler of what I considered my best music near the end of 2023, making a new one no longer seemed self-indulgent. Thus, here are fifteen of my favorite works from 2023 and 2024. This collection is over two and a half hours long, but I worked on more than ten hours of music in those two years, so it’s still a substantial distillation.

My later anthologies:

  • Iānuāriō Aprīlem MMXXV

    Latin: From January to April 2025

    A follow-up to this anthology that collects my five favorite tracks from its eponymous timespan

  • Τέττᾰρες πλευραί, ὕμνοι, καιροί, καί ἄνεμοι

    Attic Greek: Four Sides, Songs, Seasons, & Winds
    Romanized: Téttăres pleuraí, hŭ́mnoi, kairoí, kaí ắnemoi

    A later anthology of my four favorite tracks I’ve ever worked on; it’s so named because they’re all long enough to take up an LP side. (It repeats one track from this set and two from Iānuāriō Aprīlem MMXXV.)

I worked on every track here, but not every movement, and I’m only the primary composer of some of it. I sought to sequence this as an end-to-end listening experience rather than to follow anything resembling chronology, though its oldest songs appear towards the end.
