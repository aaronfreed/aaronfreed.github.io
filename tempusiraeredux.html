Tempus Irae Redux Commentary by Aaron Freed

(level designer, scripter, composer, etc.)

This page is currently a placeholder, but since Tempus Irae Redux is out, writing up thoughts on it no longer feels pointless. From its inception in May 2020 through all of 2024, Forgeflow (its director) and I were effectively the only two people actively working on it, and we’re responsible for nearly all of its changes from the original Tempus Irae and The Lost Levels. (Graphics were nearly all his work; the scripting, remastered/remixed sounds, and several original tracks were largely mine; gameplay and level changes were roughly split down the middle.)

So far, I’ve only written a page for my own level, but I may eventually write commentary for most of its levels, as I had a major role in expanding and tweaking almost all of them. (Don’t expect pages for the repositories or “It Was a Dark and Stormy Night”. “KMG-365” will almost certainly get one, though.)

Tempus Irae Redux is available through Nardo’s website ⟨nardo.bungie.org/tir⟩.
