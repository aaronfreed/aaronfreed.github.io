Tempus Irae Redux Commentary

by Aaron Freed

(level designer, composer, scripter, etc.)

This is currently a placeholder page, but with Tempus Irae Redux about to be released, it no longer feels pointless to write up my thoughts on each level. For most of its development, Forgeflow (its director) and I were the two people actively working on it, and we’re responsible for most of its changes from the original Tempus Irae and The Lost Levels. (Graphics were almost entirely his work; the scripting, sounds, and original music were largely mine; and the gameplay and level changes were roughly split down the middle.) So far, I’ve only written a page for my own level, but I eventually plan to write commentary for most of its levels, as I had a major role in expansions and tweaks to almost all of them.

Tempus Irae Redux will likely be available from Nardo’s website once it is released.

Tempus Irae Redux Music Commentary

All of my music for Tempus Irae Redux wound up on two albums, Compositions 2023-2024 (abbreviated C23-24 below) or Mūsica ex tempore malōrum (Music from a Time of Disasters, abbreviated METM below), sometimes in somewhat different forms. I’ve already written commentary for it, which I’ll link below.

  1. “Gates of Delirium”: «Θῠ́ραι παραλήρησεως» (Thúrai paralíriseōs, “Gates of Delirium”)
    «Καταιβᾰτή ἕλῐξ» (Kataibaté hélix, “Downward Spiral”), movements α & β (C23-24)
  2. “Downward Spiral”: «Ex tempore, ex locō» (“Out of Time, Out of Place”)
    “Īra temporis” (“Wrath of Time”), movement i (C23-24)
  3. “Polygonum opus”:
    1. unfinished: “Mārtiī hōrae ūndecimae” (“March of the Eleventh Hour”) (METM)
    2. finished: “Melōdia hōrae ūndecimae” (“Melody of the Eleventh Hour”) (METM)
  4. “Beyond the Black”: “You Wanted To” (C23-24)
  5. “Towel Boy”: “Fīliī Bombayānī” (“Children of Bombay”) (METM)
  6. “Gauntlet”: “Ambiēns aquātica” (“Aquatic Ambiance”) (C23-24)
  7. “The Revealing Science of God”: “Lamento Latrōnis” (“The Mercenary’s Lament”) (METM)
  8. “Game of Death”: “Ludo mortale” (“Deadly Game”) (C23-24)
  9. “…evil so singularly personified…”: “Malizia premeditata” (“Malice Aforethought”) (METM)
  10. “Il grande silenzio”:
    1. “Cauda toxica” (“Toxic Tail”) (C23-24)
    2. «Ἡ μεγᾰ́λη σιγή» (Hē megálē sigḗ, “The Great Silence”)
      «Καταιβᾰτή ἕλῐξ» (Kataibaté hélix, “Downward Spiral”), movements γ & δ (C23-24)
  11. “Mt. Vesuvius”:
    1. Finished: «Nōn omnia in suā locō corrēctō erant» (“Not Everything Was in Its Right Place”)
      “Īra temporis” (“Wrath of Time”), movement ii (C23-24)
  12. “This Is the First Day”: “Dēsīderāta” (“Things Desired”) (METM)
  13. “Big Man with a Gun”: “Lurkers in the Deep” (METM)
  14. “La fine di innocenza”: “Terminī initiaque” (“Endings and Beginnings”) (METM)
  15. “Epicus Doomicus Metallicus”: “tIq’oy’ je taHqeq” (“Heartache and the Honorless”) (intro) (METM)
