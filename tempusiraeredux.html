Tempus Irae Redux Commentary

by Aaron Freed

(level designer, composer, scripter, etc.)

This is currently a placeholder page, but with Tempus Irae Redux about to be released, it no longer feels pointless to write up my thoughts on each level. For most of its development, Forgeflow (its director) and I were the two people actively working on it, and we’re responsible for most of its changes from the original Tempus Irae and The Lost Levels. (Graphics were almost entirely his work; the scripting, sounds, and original music were largely mine; and the gameplay and level changes were roughly split down the middle.) So far, I’ve only written a page for my own level, but I eventually plan to write commentary for most of its levels.
