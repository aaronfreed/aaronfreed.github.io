What?

Partly sortable sine, cosine, and tangent values to 32 digits of precision for every integer from 0° to 359°. The sorting only sort of works right now; it doesn’t understand signs (as opposed to sines, though those too). I may fix this someday as an exercise in learning more about JavaScript, but I make no promises.

Multiples of 15° are bolded, highlighted, and printed in a different font color, while multiples of 5° are italicized, highlighted slightly less, and printed in a slightly less different font color.

Why?

I got sick of looking them up and wanted them all in one easily accessible place. I have no idea if anyone else will use it, but I created it so that I could just refer to this URL whenever I needed it, so here it is.
